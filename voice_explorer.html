About CSM Voice Explorer

This tool explores the Conversational Speech Model (CSM) from Sesame AI Labs, a state-of-the-art AI voice generation model.

Parameter Explanations

Speaker ID

Controls the voice characteristics. Different speaker IDs produce different voice styles (male/female, pitch, tone, etc.).

Temperature

Controls randomness in generation:

  • Low temperature (0.5) - More consistent, predictable, potentially monotone
  • Medium temperature (0.75-0.9) - Balanced, natural delivery
  • High temperature (1.1+) - More varied, expressive, potentially less consistent

Top-K

Controls diversity by limiting choice to top K probable tokens:

  • Low top-k (20) - More focused, less diverse word choices
  • Medium top-k (50) - Balanced word variety
  • High top-k (80+) - More diverse, creative word choices

How to Use This Explorer

Use the tabs at the top to navigate between different views:

  • All Voice Samples - Browse all generated samples with filtering options
  • Speaker Comparison - Compare different speaker IDs with the same other parameters
  • Temperature Effects - Compare the effect of different temperature values
  • TopK Effects - Compare the effect of different top-k values
